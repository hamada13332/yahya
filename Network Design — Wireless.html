Network Design

 

Wireless

Our networks are smart. you can roam around your property connected to a single WiFi network, your device will stay connected out in the garden, down the shed, in the outhouse, wherever you want it.

Security

Security for us is big, that’s why all of our systems are fully managed by us. This means we can apply the latest updates, and make security changes remotley. This keeps your equipment up to date running smoothly for years to come.

Reliability

We have been testing networking gear for the last 7 years. This has given us a broad understanding into what works, and what doesn’t. If it’s a small home or a large complex, rest assured that you will stay connected.

We can create point to point or point to multipoint wireless network links with a 2 mile range (LOS) expanding your network to where you need it most.

Remotely managed systems mean we can log-on, make changes and troubleshoot without attending site. We are also notified if you go offline.

Talk to us about what we can offer, how about battery powered redundancy for your network keeping you connected even in an outage.

 

Enterprise Solutions

We specialise in enterprise solutions for small businesses. VPNs and multiple networks across one site and ensuring your premises is fully covered with high speed, low latency broadband, so you never have to worry about internet ever again.

 

What can we do for you?

Secure client networks

Fully mananged networks with redundancy and failover to make sure you never go offline.

Point-to-point links
