Security Systems

 

In the safety of your own home, you don’t want a cloud based CCTV system, you never know who is watching and where it’s being recorded. And they rely heavily on the internet and can eat up your bandwidth. Our systems are recorded locally and stored at your property. Accessing the live feed or camera history is done over a secure link.

Security systems can be big, bulky and ugly to look at. But our camera systems use a single cable, this powers the camera and supplies the data it needs to capture and store all of your footage. With their small full metal design, built in night vision and 4k capability, you can rest assured that your property is covered, without the mess.

If you are wanting to live monitor a location without internet access we can connect your camera to a 4G connection without a hitch. Lots of our customers have out-buildings, stables or offices in close proximity. Our CCTV cameras can be extended up to 2 miles from the recorder, wirelessly. So don’t worry, we have you covered.
