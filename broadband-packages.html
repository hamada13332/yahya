Before you buy, it's good to know

Broadband isn’t just about your upload and download speeds, it is also about how responsive your broadband is. How fast does a page load once you’ve clicked on it? Even over our wireless links you can expect around 11ms latency, compared to ADSL broadband which has a national average of around 38ms latency. The lower the number, the better.
